ABOUT NICK

Nicholas Pinzon has been involved in creating music over the past couple of decades. Starting from the age of six, Nick started to learn how to play the drums; He eventually started picking up guitar, bass and ukulele over the years-combining the culmination of his musical knowledge with his love for video games. This passion led to the creation of NickyStix, his solo musical project where he covers songs from video games.

Nick's love for video game music doesn't stop there. He along with his brother Andrew, and veteran bassist Anthony Arma, also have a YouTube channel called Motion Ocean where they cover music from video games on their ukuleles.

In October 2017, Nick Pinzon entered into his newest endeavor: ArmaBanks. Joining Motion Ocean bassist Anthony Arma and his brother, Nick, alongside friend and seasoned vocalist Larry Banks, these gentlemen combine a mix of rock, soul, funk, jazz, disco, and videogame-y vibes for an experience you'll want to come back for!
